Demography of Australia



The purpose of this project was to analyze the demography of Australia covers basic statistics, most populous cities, ethnicity and religion.



As a team, we explored different sites and sources to understand Australia better. The wikipedia pages on Australia was a good guide to obtain further data. For demographics, the team used the wikipedia page and scrapped the data for further analysis. For economics and trade, the team managed to source information from the Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade.
